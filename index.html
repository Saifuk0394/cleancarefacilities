About Clean Care

Your Trusted Facility Management Partner

Clean Care is a professionally managed facility management and housekeeping service provider with over 14 years of industry experience. We deliver comprehensive housekeeping, maintenance, and support services to residential societies, commercial establishments, industrial facilities, and corporate offices.

Our success is built on integrity, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Through trained manpower, modern cleaning methodologies, and systematic supervision, we ensure clean, safe, and well-maintained environments for our clients.
