§ 01 — About

A facility management company that takes the word management seriously.

We aren't a one-time cleaning crew. We're the team your committee briefs at monthly meetings — uniformed, supervised, accountable, with a documented schedule that runs whether anyone is watching or not.

Clean Care has been keeping Mumbai's housing societies and commercial premises operating since 2012. Across fourteen years we've grown into a trusted partner for residential communities, corporate offices and industrial facilities — quietly, by doing the boring work brilliantly.

Our model is simple. Trained, background-verified manpower in uniform. Dedicated supervisors who own each site. Modern equipment we actually maintain. A scope of work documented in writing, audited weekly, and adjusted to what a property really needs — not a copy-paste contract.

Today we serve thirty-one residential communities across Powai, Chandivali, Saki Vihar Road, Sakinaka and Mulund — every one of them on the same operating standard, every one of them able to call us when something goes wrong at 11pm.

§ 02 — The Operating Rhythm

A building runs on a schedule.
So do we.

Reliable facility management isn't a one-time clean — it's a documented cadence of daily, fortnightly and periodic work that compounds into a property that quietly stays in shape. This is the schedule we run on every site we operate.

Daily
Mon — Sun · every morning
Fortnightly
Every 14 days · documented
Periodic
Monthly / quarterly · scheduled

Daily operations

07:00 – 19:00 · every day
  • 01Sweeping & mopping of lobbies, corridors and staircases
  • 02Washroom hygiene — cleaning, disinfection, consumables
  • 03Lift cabin, door and lobby cleaning
  • 04Door-to-door waste collection & segregation
  • 05Parking floor sweeping & spot cleaning
  • 06Watering plants, maintaining garden & surroundings
  • 07Supervisor checklist & issue reporting
  • 08Safety protocols, PPE compliance, signage

Fortnightly work

Every 14 days · logged
  • 01Glass and internal window cleaning

§ 05 — Onboarding

From first site visit to a property that runs itself.

A short, predictable seven-step onboarding so your committee knows exactly what to expect — and exactly when.

Step 01

Site inspection

We walk your premises with your team to understand the building, the layout and the real day-to-day requirements.

Step 02

Requirement assessment

We analyse manpower, equipment, frequencies and special needs — and identify the right operating model for the property.

Step 03

Customised proposal

You receive a written scope, schedule, headcount plan and transparent pricing — built for your property, not a template.

Step 04

Staff deployment

Trained, verified, uniformed staff are deployed on day one — with the agreed schedule already in place.

Step 05

Daily supervision

A dedicated supervisor owns your site — checklists every shift, escalations within the hour, monthly reviews on record.

Step 06

Quality audits

Surprise audits, photo logs and a written quality score — reviewed with your managing committee on a fixed cadence.

Step 07

Continuous improvement

We act on feedback — from residents, from the committee, from our own audits — and adjust the scope as the property changes.
